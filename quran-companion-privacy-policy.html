Quran Companion Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 16, 2026

Quran Companion is developed by AntJess. This privacy policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information The App Uses

Quran Companion does not require users to create an account.

The app may use approximate or precise location when you choose to use features such as prayer times and Qibla direction. Location is used for app functionality, including calculating prayer times and direction. The app does not sell your location data.

The app stores some settings and downloaded Quran audio locally on your device so the app can remember your preferences and provide offline access.

Internet And Third-Party Services

Quran Companion uses internet access to load Quran text, Quran audio, translations, and related app content from third-party services. These services may receive basic technical information required to deliver content, such as your IP address and device request information.

The app may use Google AdMob in the future to show ads. If ads are enabled, Google may collect or use device identifiers and related data according to Google's own policies.

Data Sharing

Quran Companion does not sell personal information. The app does not provide account registration and does not maintain a user profile on our servers.

Location data is used for app functionality and is not intentionally shared by us for advertising or sale.

Data Deletion

Because Quran Companion does not create user accounts or store personal profiles on our servers, there is no server-side account data to delete. You can delete local app data, preferences, and downloaded audio by clearing the app storage or uninstalling the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Quran Companion is not specifically designed for children under 13. The app is intended for a general audience.
